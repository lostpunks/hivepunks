⬡⬡⬡ HivePunks ⬡⬡⬡

HivePunks are 10,000 slightly distorted hexagonal inhabitants of the Ethereum blockchain. Each HivePunk is unique and has the exact same traits as their CryptoPunk twin, pseudo-randomly picked at the time of minting.

All metadata and images are fully generated and stored on-chain (no IPFS, no secret reveal).
